Naruto Font

Naruto Display Font is a great display font that brings out a street art vibe. This typeface has totally depended on a Japanese comic series the Naruto manga. It becomes one of the most manga series of all time.

Possessing the Truetype file format it contains 62 number of characters. Furthermore, the bold immature laters are designer with a brush, has also increased the beauty of this font.

Thanks sk89q for creating such a great font! Naruto Display Font is free for personal & commercial use. Please download and enjoy, or can search more similar fonts on befonts.
